Our Legal Practice Area

Real Estate

A widely acclaimed wing of the firm. It caters to the leading promoters, developers, contractors, investors, project financiers, and construction companies. The Firm is vastly experienced and equipped to handle the most critical real estate documentation, industrial leases, transfer of undertakings, Indentures of Conveyance, instruments relating to mortgages, creation/extinction of securities, property finances, making in-depth searches of court records, registration offices, municipal offices, and other concerned authorities, furnishing reports on title and generally advising the client on their real estate plans.

Arbitration

The firm is known for its knowledge and received tremendous appreciation from the industry stalwarts, as well as professionals for its, advise with respect to the recent trends and Amendments to the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996. We have represented clients in proceedings before various institutions across the city for Arbitration and in Ad-hoc proceedings around the country relating to various sectors including infrastructure, energy, construction, international trade, government contracts, admiralty, insurance, and product liability.

Customs and Excise

The Firm regularly represents clients in course of Customs proceedings, advises on provisions of customs rules and regulations, appears before Hearing Officers of Port and Customs, conducts writ proceedings before the High Courts and advises clients on laws governing export and import of goods. The Firm has been part of several successful Writ Petitions on behalf of container agents/shipping lines for release of marine containers abandoned or left uncleared by importers at Port premises.

Criminal

The firm handles a substantial volume of litigation in the criminal courts particularly relating to dishonour of cheques and also before High Court at Calcutta. It conducts criminal proceedings, revisional applications, and connected appeals. This includes special leave petitions and other reliefs at Supreme Court as well as High Courts.

Consumer Protection

The Firm regularly files and defends consumer complaints before the District, State and National Consumer Disputes Redressal Forum. The services of the firm include advising client, drafting petition of complaint or written objection as the case may be. Drafting affidavits of evidence and conducting trial of the cases. Counsel is engaged wherever necessary upon discussion with client.
